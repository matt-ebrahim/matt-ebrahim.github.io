About

I am a Senior Data Scientist at Formation Bio with a deep background in generative modeling and predictive AI for medical imaging and drug discovery. My work focuses on building scalable models—transformers, GNNs, diffusion, and reinforcement learning—to generate novel, biologically meaningful molecules. I lead multimodal fusion projects integrating molecular graphs, quantum chemistry, and biological assay data.
